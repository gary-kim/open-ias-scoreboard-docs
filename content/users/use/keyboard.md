## This page will detail keyboard mappings for Open IAS Scoreboard

### Keyboard combinations

- Scoreboard Tabs
    - New Tab: `ctrl+t`
    - Next Tab: `ctrl+tab`
    - Previous Tab: `ctrl+shift+tab`
    - Close Tab: `ctrl+w`
- Scoreboard Controls
    - Home
        - Increase Score: `i`
        - Decrease Score: `k`
    - Guest
        - Increase Score: `o`
        - Decrease Score: `l`
    - Clock
        - Toggle Clock: `t`
        - Increase 1 Second: `u`
        - Decrease 1 Second: `j`
